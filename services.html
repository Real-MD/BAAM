Products & Services

What We Supply

BAAM Materials Ltd supplies quality construction materials direct to site. We work with small to mid-size builders and contractors who need fast, reliable supply without the hassle of merchant queues or credit accounts. All orders operate on a proforma basis — cleared funds, fast turnaround, straight to your site.

Aggregates & Bulk Bags

from £45 per bag

Aggregates & Bulk Bags

We supply a full range of aggregates in convenient bulk bags, delivered direct to your site on a flat-bed or hiab vehicle. Whether you need sand for bedding, gravel for drainage, or sharp sand for concrete mixing, we have you covered.

Bulk bag quantities mean less waste, easier handling, and a cleaner site. Ideal for groundworks, drainage runs, and general construction use.

  • Sharp sand (concreting / plastering)
  • Building sand (soft / bricklaying)
  • 10mm & 20mm gravel / shingle
  • MOT Type 1 sub-base
  • Topsoil & fill material
  • Ballast (combined aggregate)

Pricing from £45 per bulk bag — contact Martin for specific material pricing and multi-bag discounts.

Cement & Binders

We stock a range of cement and binder products suitable for all stages of construction. From standard OPC cement for general mixing to postcrete for fast fence post installation — supplied in manageable 25kg bags on pallets for easy site unloading.

Competitive pricing and fast delivery means you can keep your trades moving without waiting on a merchant's next delivery slot.

  • Ordinary Portland Cement (OPC) 25kg
  • Mastercrete / general purpose cement 25kg
  • Postcrete rapid-set fence post mix 20kg
  • Pre-mixed sand & cement 20kg bags
  • Lime for mortar and plaster
  • Waterproof admixtures available on request

OPC cement from £5.25 per bag — pallet quantities available at improved pricing.

Structural Timber

We can supply in bundle quantities for larger projects or individual lengths for smaller jobs — always at competitive pricing without merchant queue times.

  • 6x2 (47x150mm) C24 regularised
  • 8x2 (47x200mm) C24 floor joists
  • 3x2 (47x75mm) studwork timber
  • Treated / tanalised options available
  • Bespoke lengths cut to order on request

4x2 C24 from £1.75 per metre — bulk lengths and packs available with better rates.
